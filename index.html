About Betel Leaf

Betel leaf (Piper betle) is an aromatic, heart-shaped leaf used widely in Asia. Diseases strongly affect its commercial value — hence realtime detection helps farmers prevent losses.

Dataset & Disease Types

  • BacterialLeafSpot_Green – bacterial water-soaked spots
  • Healthy_Green – disease-free green leaf
  • Healthy_Red – normal red variety leaf
